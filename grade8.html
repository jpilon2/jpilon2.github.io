GRADE 8
In grade 6 students work with the apple software iMovie to create their first video productions. At the core of the class lies the design cycle. Students need to thoroughly research, plan and test their creation. They need to create storyboards and consider things such as camera angles, sound effects, audio tracks, props, costumes, script, and much more. There are two main projects in the class. The Silent movie project and the Time Lapse/Reverse video.
